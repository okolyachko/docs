Alan AI cheat sheet
-------------------

Even if you have been designing AI assistants with the Alan AI Platform for a while, it is hard to memorize all dialog script concepts and methods by heart.

To let you quickly recall how to use this or that means or tool, we have prepared Alan AI cheat sheet. The cheat sheet covers all major script concepts, client API methods, handlers and more.
