	  <p style="font-size: 28px; font-weight: 700">1. Create an Alan AI Studio account</p>

      <p style="font-size: 28px; font-weight: 700">2. Write a dialog script</p>
	  
   In Alan Studio, write a dialog script. Dialog scripts describe the structure of the dialog with the user, or the expected conversation scenario.

   Get started swiftly:
   
	
   - Go to :doc:`Dialog script concepts <server-api/script-concepts>` to learn what Alan AI's functionality you can use to create a dialog script.

   - Check out `Example scripts <https://github.com/alan-ai/voice-assistant-scripts>`__ in Alan AI Studio to see how dialog scripts can be organized.
   
   
   
.. raw:: html
   
   <p style="font-size: 28px; font-weight: 700; text-align:center">3. Integrate the AI assistant with your app</p>
   
Alan AI offers SDKs for different platforms to embed an AI assistant to your app. Select your platform to learn about :doc:`integration details <client-api/integration>`. Upon integration, you'll see a small draggable button on top of the app's UI.

	  <p style="font-size: 28px; font-weight: 700">4. Analyze and iterate</p>
	  
   Alan AI provides you with advanced conversational analytics and success metrics through its :doc:`Logs <usage/guides/logs>` and :doc:`Analytics View<usage/guides/analytics-view>`. Use these tools to:

   - Gain insight into the users' experience
   - Analyze their behavior and flows
   - Identify unhandled requests 
   - Optimize the AI assistant effectiveness
