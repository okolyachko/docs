1. Set up an Alan AI Studio account
-----------------------------------

`Sign up for Alan AI Studio <https://studio.alan.app/register>`__, a web-based IDE from Alan AI. Alan AI Studio  provides a complete toolkit for creating an AI assistant project, testing and debugging the dialog, customizing and deploying your assistant.

|
   
2. Define your datasets and commands
------------------------------------

In Alan Studio, write a dialog script for your AI assistant. To get started quickly:

- :doc:`Define static and dynamic data corpuses <server-api/qna>` that will be used as knowledge sources to answer users' queries
- :doc:`Add text and voice commands <server-api/commands-and-responses>` for the AI assistant to handle

.. image:: /_static/assets/img/about/get-started-scripts.png 

|

3. Use transforms to fine-tune queries and output
-------------------------------------------------

Add :doc:`transforms </server-api/transforms/overview>` to define what data corpuses to query and fine-tune AI assistant's responses.
 
4. Integrate the AI assistant into your app
-------------------------------------------

Alan AI provides SDKs for several platforms, frameworks and languages to embed an AI assistant to your app. To learn about :doc:`integration details <client-api/integration>`, select your platform from the list below. Upon integration, you'll see a small draggable button on top of the app's UI.

5. Choose your AI assistant type
--------------------------------

With Alan AI, you can:

- :doc:`Add an AI-powered chat <usage/button/chat>` to you app
- :doc:`Create voice AI assistant <usage/button/customization>` for you app

Choose your AI assistant type and change the way your AI assistant looks and feels using :doc:`customization options <usage/button/customization>`.

.. image:: /_static/assets/img/about/get-started-customize.png 

|

6. Analyze and iterate
----------------------
	  
Alan AI offers advanced  :doc:`conversational analytics and success metrics <usage/guides/analytics-view>`. Use these tools to:

- Gain insight into the users' conversational experience
- Analyze their behavior and flows
- Identify unhandled requests 
- Optimize and enhance the AI assistant

.. image:: /_static/assets/img/about/get-started-analytics.png
